My Story So Far...

Though born in Washington, my family moved to Idaho where I graduated from Rigby High School, in Rigby Idaho. With a deep desire to see the world, I was honored to have served in the U.S. Air Force as an Avionics Technician on the F-15E. After my military career, I continued to travel the country as a telecommunications technician. It was during this timeI helped build the infrastructure for Google Fiber in Provo, UT. Ever the jack of all trades, I pursued work in both the construction and remodeling markets in various roles, including siding, plumbing and carpentry.

Then enters the great pandemic of 2020. When I was laid off from my job, I decided that it was time to achieve my longtime goal of becoming a web developer. I have always had a passion for all things computer and technology related. I worked hard and studied from every resource that I could glean information from, and am having fun soaking up any information that I can. Along the way I have completed two certificate programs (so far) through freeCodeCamp.org.
